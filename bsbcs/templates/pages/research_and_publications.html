Publication Submission Guidelines

Share your research with the BSBCS community

1. Manuscript Preparation

  • Follow ICMJE (International Committee of Medical Journal Editors) formatting standards
  • Include structured abstract (250 words maximum) with Background, Methods, Results, Conclusions
  • Provide 3-6 keywords using MeSH (Medical Subject Headings) terminology

2. Ethical Requirements

  • Include IRB/Ethics Committee approval documentation for human subjects research
  • Provide informed consent statements and patient confidentiality assurances
  • Disclose all funding sources and potential conflicts of interest

3. Peer Review Process

  • Double-blind peer review by minimum two independent reviewers with relevant expertise
  • Average review timeline: 4-6 weeks from submission to initial decision
  • Authors receive detailed feedback and revision recommendations
