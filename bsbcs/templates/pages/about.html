Our Journey of Excellence

Milestones that shaped breast cancer care in Bangladesh

2010 Foundation

Society Established

Founded by 50 pioneering oncologists committed to advancing breast cancer care in Bangladesh through collaborative research and education.

2013 Research

First National Research Initiative

Launched comprehensive multi-center study on breast cancer prevalence and treatment outcomes across Bangladesh, establishing baseline data for future research.

2016 Recognition

International Partnership

Established formal collaboration with American Society of Clinical Oncology (ASCO), bringing international expertise to Bangladesh.

2019 Education

National Training Program

Launched comprehensive training program for healthcare professionals, reaching 1,000+ medical practitioners across Bangladesh.

2022 Innovation

AI Research Initiative

Pioneered AI-powered early detection research, achieving 95% accuracy in mammography interpretation and setting new standards for diagnostic excellence.

2025 Present

Leading Regional Authority

Now serving 2,500+ members with 1,200+ research publications, recognized as South Asia's premier breast cancer research and education society.
